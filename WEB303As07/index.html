Who is Towelie?

Towelie is a fictional character from the animated TV series South Park. He is a talking towel known for his catchphrase "Wanna get high?" Towelie is known for his love of cannabis and often gets involved in various misadventures with the boys in South Park.

Towelie's Adventures

Towelie has been featured in several South Park episodes, including "Towelie," "A Million Little Fibers," and "Crippled Summer." In these episodes, he finds himself in humorous and often absurd situations, providing comic relief to the show's storylines.

Towelie is a light blue towel with arms and legs. He has a red "T" on his chest and large eyes. He is often seen with a joint or a blunt in his mouth.

Towelie is known for his carefree and stoned personality. He often gets high and forgets important details, leading to comical situations.

His famous catchphrase, "Wanna get high?" is a central part of his character and is frequently used in the show.
